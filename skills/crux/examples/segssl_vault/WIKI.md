### comparison

- [[segmentation-decoders\|Segmentation Decoder Architectures (SegFormer, Segmenter, U-Net, DeepLabv3+)]] — Comparative page on the four decoder/head families SegSSL pairs with pretrained encoders: lightweight MLP (SegFormer), transformer mask decoder (Segmenter), convolutional encoder-decoder with skips (U-Net), and atrous-conv with decoder refinement (DeepLabv3+).

### concept

- [[contrastive-learning\|Contrastive Learning (SSL)]] — SSL family that learns representations by pulling augmented views of the same image together and pushing different images apart, spanning image-level (SimCLR, MoCo) and dense/pixel-level (DenseCL, PixPro) formulations.
- [[label-efficiency\|Label-Efficient Transfer to Dense Prediction]] — Cross-cutting evaluation axis in SegSSL measuring how few labeled masks (linear probe, few-shot, low-data fine-tuning) are needed after SSL pretraining to reach strong segmentation accuracy on ADE20K/Cityscapes/VOC.
- [[masked-image-modeling\|Masked Image Modeling (MIM)]] — SSL family that masks patches and trains a model to reconstruct pixels (MAE), discrete tokens (BEiT), or online-teacher targets (iBOT), producing encoders with strong localized/dense feature sensitivity.
- [[self-distillation\|Self-Distillation (SSL)]] — SSL family where a student network matches a momentum-averaged teacher's output on differently augmented views with no explicit negatives, spanning DINO, DINOv2, and (in its masked-patch form) iBOT.

### dataset

- [[cityscapes\|Cityscapes Dataset]] — Urban driving-scene segmentation benchmark (fine/coarse annotations, 19 classes) used both as an in-domain fine-tuning target and as the source-domain anchor for the ACDC domain-shift evaluation in SegSSL.

### entity

- [[vision-transformer\|Vision Transformer (ViT) Backbone]] — Patch-based transformer encoder (ViT-B/16, Dosovitskiy et al. 2020) that serves as the shared backbone every SSL method in SegSSL pretrains and every decoder fine-tunes.

### method

- [[beit\|BEiT]] — BERT-style masked image modeling that predicts discrete visual tokens from a dVAE tokenizer for masked patches, an alternative masked-modeling formulation to MAE's pixel reconstruction.
- [[densecl\|DenseCL]] — Extends contrastive pretraining from single global image vectors to dense, pixel/region-level correspondence matching, directly targeting representations useful for dense prediction tasks like segmentation.
- [[dino\|DINO (Self-Distillation with No Labels)]] — Student-teacher self-distillation with a momentum teacher plus centering/sharpening that trains ViT to produce emergent, segmentation-friendly attention maps without labels or negatives.
- [[dinov2\|DINOv2]] — Scaled-up, curated-data successor to DINO combining self-distillation with an iBOT-style masked-patch loss to produce general-purpose, frozen-feature-ready ViT representations.
- [[ibot\|iBOT]] — Unifies masked image modeling and self-distillation via an online tokenizer (a momentum teacher) supplying targets for masked-patch prediction, bridging BEiT-style MIM and DINO-style distillation.
- [[mae\|Masked Autoencoders (MAE)]] — Asymmetric encoder-decoder that reconstructs heavily masked (75%) image patches in pixel space, yielding strong ViT initializations for dense prediction with minimal labeled fine-tuning.
- [[moco\|MoCo (Momentum Contrast)]] — Contrastive learning with a queue-based memory bank and momentum-updated key encoder that decouples negative-sample count from batch size, making contrastive pretraining batch-efficient.
- [[pixpro\|PixPro (Pixel-to-Propagation)]] — Pixel-level self-supervised pretraining using a pixel-propagation module and consistency across overlapping crop regions, another dense-prediction-oriented contrastive variant alongside DenseCL.
- [[simclr\|SimCLR]] — Foundational contrastive framework using strong augmentations, a projection head, and the InfoNCE/NT-Xent loss to pull augmented views of the same image together in embedding space.

### overview

- [[overview\|SegSSL: Label-Efficient Semantic Segmentation via Self-Supervised Pretraining]] — Research-program hub mapping how contrastive, masked-image-modeling, and self-distillation SSL pretrain ViT-B/16 encoders that transfer to segmentation decoders under label scarcity and domain shift.
